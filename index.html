About Tamil Nadu Youth Congress

Committed to Youth Empowerment and Social Progress

Our Mission

Tamil Nadu Youth Congress serves as the dynamic youth wing of the Indian National Congress in Tamil Nadu. We are dedicated to empowering young people, promoting democratic values, and working towards social justice and equality for all.

Our Vision

To create a Tamil Nadu where every young person has access to quality education, meaningful employment, and opportunities to contribute to society's progress while upholding the secular and democratic principles of our nation.

Our Values

We stand firmly committed to protecting democratic institutions, ensuring electoral integrity, and fighting for the rights of every citizen. Through our various initiatives including the VOTE CHORI campaign, we work tirelessly to safeguard democracy and empower the youth of Tamil Nadu.
